Delete Your Raze Account

To request deletion of your Raze account and all associated data, please email us at razefitnessapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

Please include your registered email address in the request. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days of receiving your request.

Data that will be deleted:

  • Account information (name, email address)
  • Health and fitness data (height, weight, age, activity level)
  • Workout logs and exercise history
  • Food logs and nutrition data
  • App preferences and settings

Data that may be retained:

  • Purchase records (retained by Google Play per their policies)
  • Anonymized analytics data

For questions, contact us at razefitnessapp@gmail.com
